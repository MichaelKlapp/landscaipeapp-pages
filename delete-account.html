To delete your LandscAIpe account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

Steps to Request Account Deletion

Step 1: Open your email app or webmail
Step 2: Compose a new email to: privacy@landscaipe.com
Step 3: Include the following information:
  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Your email address associated with your LandscAIpe account
  • A clear statement that you want to delete your account
Step 4: Send the email and wait for confirmation (typically within 7 business days)

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, we will permanently delete the following data associated with your account:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, phone number, ZIP code
  • Authentication Data: User ID, encrypted password
  • Project Data: All landscaping projects, design preferences, budget information, project notes
  • Photos: All photos you uploaded to the app for landscape design purposes
  • Design History: All AI-generated design visualizations and variations
  • Cost Estimates: All cost estimates and project recommendations
  • App Activity Data: Analytics events and interaction history associated with your account

What Data May Be Retained

For legal and business purposes, we may retain certain information:

  • Transaction Records: If you made any purchases through the app, we may retain financial transaction records as required by law (typically 7 years for tax purposes)
  • Anonymized Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized analytics data that cannot be linked to your identity may be retained for service improvement
  • Crash Logs: Anonymous crash logs and diagnostics that do not contain personal identifiers

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 7 business days of receiving your request. You will receive an email confirmation once your account and data have been deleted.

Important Notes

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted, you will not be able to recover any data or restore your account.
  • If you have active projects or pending transactions, these will be cancelled when your account is deleted.
  • After account deletion, you will need to create a new account if you wish to use LandscAIpe again in the future.

Contact Us

If you have questions about account deletion or need assistance with the process, please contact us:

Email: privacy@landscaipe.com
Company: LandscAIpe, LLC
Response Time: We aim to respond to all requests within 7 business days
