Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 29, 2025

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Photos You Upload

LandscAIpe uses your device's camera and photo library to capture and upload images of your yard or property. We process these images using AI technology to generate landscape design ideas and cost estimates.

1.2 Location Data

We may collect location data to improve estimates and match you with local contractors. You can control this through your device settings.

1.3 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Name (optional)
  • Password (encrypted and stored securely)

1.4 Project Information

We collect information you provide about your landscaping project, including:

  • Property type and size
  • Desired features and style preferences
  • Budget range
  • Change level preference
  • Descriptions about your yard or project

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Generate AI-powered landscape design visualizations based on your photos and preferences
  • Provide cost estimates and project recommendations
  • Save your projects and design history
  • Connect you with local contractors only when you agree to share your project details
  • Improve our services and AI technology
  • Communicate with you about your account and projects
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the app

3. AI Processing and Third-Party Services

Your photos are processed using Google's Gemini AI technology through secure server-side APIs. We do not share your photos with any other third parties except as necessary to provide our services.

4. Sharing of Information

  • Contractors: Your project details are shared only when you agree to share them
  • Service Providers: Vendors who help us run the app; they must protect your information
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and security practices. We use Supabase for secure data storage and authentication. Photos are temporarily processed for AI generation and stored in your project history. We use reasonable safeguards to protect information, but no system is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. You can request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting us at the email address below.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt out of certain data uses

8. Permissions We Request

LandscAIpe requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: To take photos of your yard for landscaping estimates
  • Photo Library: To upload existing photos of your property for landscaping estimates

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
