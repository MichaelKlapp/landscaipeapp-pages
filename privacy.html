Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 5, 2026
Last Updated: January 5, 2026

Definitions

Service means the LandscAIpe mobile application and related services.

Personal Data means information that can identify you directly or indirectly.

Usage Data means information collected automatically from your use of the Service (for example, event activity and diagnostic data).

User Content means photos, text, project details, and other content you submit to the Service.

Service Providers means third parties we use to operate the Service on our behalf.

Contractors means independent third parties who may receive Lead information if you choose to submit it.

Lead means a request you submit to selected contractors that includes contact and project details.

Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve the Service.

Personal Data

When you create an account or update your profile, we may collect:

  • Email address (required)
  • Password (required; stored and managed by our authentication provider)
  • ZIP code (required)
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Display name (optional)
  • Profile photo (optional)

User Content and Project Data

Depending on how you use the Service, we may collect and store:

  • Yard or property photos you take in the app
  • Photos you upload from your device photo library
  • Plant photos (for plant tools)
  • AI-generated landscape design images created from your inputs
  • Favorites and saved items
  • Project details such as project type, features, budget range, style preferences, change level, dimensions, notes, and selections

Lead Submission Data

If you choose to submit a Lead to contractors, we may collect the information you provide, which can include:

  • Name
  • Email
  • Phone number
  • Address (optional)
  • ZIP code
  • Project summary details (type, features, budget range)
  • Project notes (free-form text)
  • Photos you choose to include (original photos and or generated design images)
  • Contact preferences (email, phone, either, best time to contact)
  • Which contractors you selected to receive the Lead

Usage Data

We may collect Usage Data to monitor and improve the Service. This can include:

  • Event activity (app opens, onboarding completion, screen views, button taps, design generations, design selections, subscription actions)
  • Device identifiers (such as iOS IDFV, Android device ID, and or an app-generated identifier)
  • Session identifiers and timestamps
  • Basic device information and diagnostic context related to app behavior and errors

We do not run advertising, remarketing, or cross-app tracking via advertising SDKs.

Location Data

We do not request GPS or location permissions in the app. Instead, we collect your ZIP code and may derive approximate location context (such as city, state, and ZIP-derived coordinates) to display weather information, provide location-aware context for certain features, and support contractor matching where applicable.

Tracking Technologies (Website) and Local Device Storage (App)

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies. Our mobile app may use local device storage (for example, to store settings, in-progress project flow data, and identifiers used for analytics and reliability).

Use of Data

We use the collected data for purposes that include:

  • Providing and maintaining the Service
  • Creating and managing your account and authentication
  • Enabling core features (projects, favorites, saved items, shopping lists)
  • Generating AI designs and powering AI tools you request
  • Delivering Leads to the contractors you selected when you submit a Lead
  • Providing customer support
  • Monitoring usage, reliability, and performance
  • Detecting, preventing, and addressing technical issues and abuse
  • Improving the Service and user experience

AI Processing

The Service includes AI features such as landscape design generation, Yard Nerd chat, and plant tools.

When you use these features, we may send relevant inputs to our AI provider, such as:

  • Photos you upload (encoded for transmission)
  • Prompts or questions you enter
  • Project preferences (budget, style, features)
  • ZIP-derived location context (city and state)

AI outputs are generated automatically and may be inaccurate, incomplete, or not suitable for your situation. You are responsible for how you use AI outputs.

Retention of Data

We retain Personal Data and User Content only for as long as necessary to provide the Service and for legitimate business purposes (such as security, dispute resolution, and compliance).

Usage Data is generally retained for shorter periods, except where it is used to strengthen security, improve functionality, or where we are legally obligated to retain it longer.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account in the app via Account Settings, then Delete Account, then Confirm.

When you delete your account, we aim to delete:

  • Files associated with your account (such as profile photos, favorite images, generated images)
  • Associated database records tied to your user ID (such as profile, favorites, shopping lists, subscription records, Leads, and related audit records)
  • Your authentication record

Subscriptions: Deleting your account does not automatically cancel subscriptions. Subscriptions must be canceled through your Apple App Store or Google Play settings, and access may continue until the end of the billing period.

Transfer of Data

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction, where data protection laws may differ.

By using the Service and submitting information, you consent to such transfers.

We may share information with Service Providers who help us operate the Service. These providers have access to information only to perform tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to use it for other purposes.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (for example, a court or government agency), or to protect rights, safety, and security.

Security of Data

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect your information, including encryption in transit (HTTPS) and access controls. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100 percent secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We store photos and images in private storage and use signed access where applicable.

Do Not Track Signals (Website)

Our website may not respond to Do Not Track signals. You can enable or disable Do Not Track by visiting your browser settings.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or request a copy of your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing.

You can update certain account and profile information within the Service, and you can delete your account in-app. For other requests, contact us using the information below. We may ask you to verify your identity before responding.

We do not knowingly collect Personal Data from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected Personal Data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete it. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided Personal Data, please contact us.
