Definitions

Usage Data means information collected automatically from your use of the Service (for example, event activity and diagnostic data).

Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve the Service.

Account and Profile Data

When you create an account or update your profile, we may collect:

  • Email address (required)
  • Password (required; stored and managed by Supabase Auth)
  • ZIP code (required)
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Full name (optional; may be provided via Apple Sign-In)
  • Profile photo (optional)

Account information is cached locally on your device and stored server-side in Supabase.

Project Data

Depending on how you use the Service, we may collect and store:

  • Project details: type, descriptions, dimensions, budget, and style preferences
  • Progress photos of your yard or property
  • AI-generated landscape design images
  • Checklist items, materials lists, shopping lists, and saved tutorials
  • Favorites and saved items

Project data is stored in Supabase.

Location Data

The app requests foreground device location permission to auto-fill your ZIP code, which is used for regional plant suggestions, local tips, and weather context. Raw GPS coordinates are not stored; only your ZIP code is retained. Location access is collected only when you grant permission and can be revoked at any time through your device settings. If a consent modal is shown, collection is subject to your consent choice.

Photos and Images

Yard photos, plant photos, and profile photos you upload are stored in private Supabase Storage. AI-generated design images are also stored in private Supabase Storage. Images are accessed via time-limited signed URLs and are not publicly accessible.

Push Notification Tokens

If you enable push notifications, we store your push token, device platform, app version, and last-seen timestamp in Supabase to deliver notifications. You can disable push notifications at any time through your device settings.

Analytics and Device Data

We may collect device and usage data to monitor and improve the Service, including:

  • App version, build number, and platform (iOS or Android)
  • Session ID and anonymous install ID (stored locally on your device)
  • Screen navigation events (which screens were viewed)
  • In-app actions and feature usage (see Analytics and Tracking below)

We do not use advertising SDKs, remarketing SDKs, or cross-app tracking. No advertising identifiers are collected, and no App Tracking Transparency prompt is shown.

Lead and Quote Request Data

If you choose to request contractor quotes, we collect the information you provide, which may include:

  • Name, email, phone number, and ZIP code
  • Project summary, type, budget, and notes
  • Photos you choose to include (yard photos and AI-generated designs)
  • Contact preferences and which contractors you selected

This information is shared with the contractors you select when you submit a request.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected data for purposes including:

  • Creating and managing your account and authentication
  • Providing core features: projects, favorites, shopping lists, checklists, materials, and tutorials
  • Generating AI landscape designs from your photos and preferences
  • Providing plant identification, plant health diagnosis, and plant search features
  • Matching you with local contractors when you request quotes
  • Sending project-related push notifications and maintenance reminders
  • Personalizing design suggestions based on your region and season
  • Processing in-app subscription purchases and verifying entitlements
  • Sending transactional emails (welcome, password reset, lead notifications)
  • Sending re-engagement notifications
  • Improving the Service through aggregated, anonymized analytics
  • Detecting, preventing, and addressing technical issues and abuse

Analytics and Tracking

PostHog

We use PostHog for behavioral analytics, hosted in the United States. PostHog tracks named in-app events including screen views, design generation, feature usage, checklist actions, lead flow, subscription events, and errors. When you are logged in, your user ID is attached to events; before login, an anonymous ID is used. Events may also be mirrored to an internal Supabase table.

PII filtering: email addresses, passwords, phone numbers, addresses, full images, GPS coordinates, payment details, and card numbers are stripped before sending to PostHog. ZIP codes are truncated to the first 3 digits before transmission.

If a consent modal is shown, you may opt out of analytics through that modal.

Sentry

We use Sentry for crash reporting, error monitoring, and performance tracing. Session replay is enabled, with approximately 10% of sessions sampled and 100% of error sessions captured. An optional in-app feedback form allows you to submit your name, email, and a description of an issue; screenshots are disabled in the feedback form. Sentry's configuration may collect additional identifying context per Sentry's documentation. Session and error data is hosted by Sentry.io.

Website Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics. Our website may also use cookies or similar technologies. You can adjust tracking through your browser settings. Our website may not respond to Do Not Track signals.

AI Processing

The Service includes AI features including landscape design generation, the Yard Nerd chat assistant, plant identification, plant health diagnosis, and plant search. When you use these features, relevant inputs are sent to our backend, which uses Google Gemini AI to generate outputs. Inputs may include:

  • Photos you upload (encoded for transmission)
  • Prompts, questions, or descriptions you enter
  • Project preferences (budget, style, features)
  • ZIP-derived location context (city and state)

User photos sent for AI processing are transmitted securely and outputs are stored in your private project storage. Content submitted for AI generation is subject to Google's usage policies. AI outputs are generated automatically and may be inaccurate, incomplete, or not suitable for your situation. You are responsible for how you use AI outputs, including plant identification results used for health or safety decisions.

Authentication and Sign-In

The Service supports the following sign-in methods, all managed through Supabase Auth:

  • Email and password: Managed by Supabase Auth. Password reset links are sent by email and redirect to https://landscaipeapp.com/reset-password.html.
  • Sign in with Google: Exchanges a Google ID token with Supabase. Scopes requested are profile and email.
  • Sign in with Apple: Exchanges an Apple ID token with Supabase. Scopes requested are name and email.

In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

DIY Pro is an auto-renewing subscription available through the Apple App Store (iOS) and Google Play Store (Android). Purchase receipts and transaction tokens are sent to our backend for verification; we do not store raw payment card information. Subscription status is verified server-side and cached locally. Stripe is used for contractor-side billing and is not used for in-app mobile purchases. Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your subscription; subscriptions must be canceled through your Apple App Store or Google Play settings.

Data Sharing with Third Parties

We share data with the following third parties as necessary to operate the Service:

  • Supabase - Database, storage, authentication, and edge functions. All user and project data is processed and stored here.
  • Google - Sign-in via OAuth (Google ID token) and AI generation via Google Gemini on the backend. Yard photos and prompts are sent server-side for AI processing.
  • Apple - Sign-in via OAuth (Apple ID token).
  • PostHog - Behavioral analytics. Anonymized usage events and an anonymous or user ID are shared.
  • Sentry - Error reporting, session replay, and optional user feedback. Crash data and session recordings are shared.
  • Expo - Over-the-air app updates and push notification delivery. Push token and app metadata are shared.
  • Matched Contractors - Only when you initiate a quote request. Name, email, phone, ZIP, and project details are shared with the contractors you select.
  • Apple App Store and Google Play - Subscription processing.
  • Open-Meteo - Weather data. ZIP-derived location context is used to fetch weather information.

We do not sell your personal information. We may also share information in connection with a business transaction (such as a merger, acquisition, or asset sale), in response to legal requirements, or to protect rights, safety, and security.

Security of Data

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect your information, including encryption in transit (HTTPS) and access controls. Photos and images are stored in private storage and accessed via time-limited signed URLs. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100 percent secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention and Account Deletion

Account data is retained until you delete your account. You can delete your account in the app via Account Settings, then Delete Account, then Confirm. When you delete your account, we delete:

  • Files associated with your account (profile photos, project photos, generated images)
  • Database records tied to your user ID (profile, projects, favorites, shopping lists, subscription records, Leads, and related records)
  • Your authentication record
  • Your push notification tokens (flagged inactive on deletion)

Analytics events are retained per PostHog's and Sentry's respective data retention policies. Usage Data is generally retained for shorter periods, except where needed for security, functionality, or legal compliance.

Subscriptions: Deleting your account does not automatically cancel subscriptions. You must cancel through your Apple App Store or Google Play settings. Access may continue until the end of the current billing period.
