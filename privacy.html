Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 17, 2025

LANDSCAIPE LLC (“LandscAIpe,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) provides an AI‑powered mobile application that helps users generate landscaping cost estimates, design ideas, and connect with contractors.

1. Information We Collect

  • Photos & Descriptions you provide about a yard or project.
  • Location Data to improve estimates and match local contractors.
  • Contact Information (name, email, phone) if you create an account or join our waitlist.
  • Usage Data to improve performance and reliability.

2. How We Use Information

  • Generate AI‑based landscaping estimates and design suggestions.
  • Connect you with local contractors only with your consent.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the app.

3. Sharing of Information

  • Contractors: shared only when you agree to share your project details.
  • Service Providers: vendors who help us run the app (e.g., cloud hosting); they must protect your information.
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information, but no system is 100% secure.

5. Children’s Privacy

LandscAIpe is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will post updates with a new “Last Updated” date.
